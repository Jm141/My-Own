My Poem

In a heart once broken, love does reignite, A soul awakens, shining ever so bright. Through the darkest night, a glimmer finds its way, A person finds love, once again, they say.

With hesitant steps, they take a chance, Leaving behind scars of a failed romance. But fate has other plans, a story to rewrite, Love blooms anew, casting away the night.

A tender touch, a smile that melts the heart, A connection deep, like a timeless work of art. In their embrace, a healing balm is found, Love's sweet whispers, a symphony of sound.

Together they journey, hand in hand, Creating memories, a future they've planned. With gratitude in their eyes, they've learned to see, Love's second chance, a gift meant to be.

Poem 2

In times of joy or sorrow's tide, A friend who's always by my side. With warmth and care, their presence near, A beacon of light, so crystal clear.

Through laughter shared and tears we weep, Their steadfast love runs deep and steep. With every step, they walk along, A bond unbreakable, forever strong.

Through thick and thin, they're always there, A friend who shows me how to bear. In life's embrace, we dance and sway, Together, we'll conquer come what may.

Poem 3

A tale unfolds of a heart's hushed clutch. Silent whispers, unspoken desire, A soul that yearns, consumed by fire.

In hidden glances, a love takes flight, Unveiling its wings in the dark of night. Fear's icy grip, a binding refrain, Conceals the words, the heart would explain.

Behind a mask of uncertain fears, A tender heart weeps unspoken tears. Yet, in silence, a love blooms strong, Unseen melodies, a secret song.

In dreams of whispers, love's notes arise, Soft melodies that light up the skies. Though he may fear to let words take flight, Love's ardor burns, relentless and bright.

Oh, let him know, in your quiet way, That love's flame flickers, will not betray. For love's true essence can still transcend, The barriers that silence intends.

So, in your heart, let courage arise, Unveil the truth, and watch as it flies. For love's embrace can bring solace true, When two souls unite, love's whispers ensue.
